# Vision

FIXAR OS will become the digital brain of the entire factory.

Every machine, employee, material, customer, supplier, production order and financial transaction will be managed from a single platform.

The long-term vision is to build one of the world's most advanced AI-powered manufacturing operating systems for polyurethane insole production.
