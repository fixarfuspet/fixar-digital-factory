# Purpose

This document defines the Artificial Intelligence architecture of FIXAR OS.

Artificial Intelligence is not an external assistant.

AI is a native layer inside FIXAR OS that continuously monitors, analyzes, predicts and assists every department.

AI never replaces people.

AI supports better decision making.

---

# AI Vision

FIXAR OS is designed as an AI-first ERP/MES platform.

Every business event becomes data.

Every data point becomes knowledge.

Every knowledge becomes recommendation.

Every recommendation helps humans make faster and better decisions.

---

# AI Core Principles

- AI assists humans.
- AI never performs critical actions automatically.
- Every recommendation is explainable.
- Every recommendation has a confidence score.
- Every recommendation is traceable.
- Every AI decision is logged.
- Every AI model can be improved continuously.

# AI Business Rules

## AI-BR-001

AI cannot execute critical business actions.

## AI-BR-002

Every recommendation must have confidence score.

## AI-BR-003

Every recommendation must be explainable.

## AI-BR-004

Every AI interaction must be logged.

## AI-BR-005

AI must respect user permissions.

## AI-BR-006

AI only accesses authorized business data.

## AI-BR-007

AI recommendations never bypass approval workflows.

## AI-BR-008

AI models must be version controlled.

## AI-BR-009

Rejected recommendations remain available for learning analysis.

## AI-BR-010

Every AI response must be traceable to its supporting data.
