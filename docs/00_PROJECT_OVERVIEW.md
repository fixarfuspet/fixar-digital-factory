# Vision

FIXAR OS is an AI-powered Digital Factory Operating System designed specifically for polyurethane insole manufacturing.

The platform combines ERP, MES, WMS, CRM, Quality Management, Maintenance, Finance, Artificial Intelligence and Industrial IoT into a single integrated system.

The objective is to transform FIXAR into a fully digital, data-driven and AI-assisted manufacturing company.

---

# Mission

Build the world's most advanced operating system for polyurethane insole manufacturing.

Enable every production decision to be based on real-time data.

Provide complete traceability from raw material to customer.

Increase productivity while reducing waste, downtime and operational costs.
